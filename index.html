Why choose us

We built the grocery run we always wished existed.

Harvestly started as a single market stall in 2013. Today we still buy directly from growers each morning — no distributors, no cold storage for weeks. It's just a shorter, honest supply chain.

  • 🌱

    Farm-direct sourcing

    We work with 120+ regional farms, cutting out middlemen so produce reaches you within 24 hours of harvest.

  • 🚚

    90-minute delivery

    Order before 8pm and our cold-chain riders get it to your door same evening, temperature-controlled.

  • 🔍

    Full traceability

    Every crate is tagged with the farm it came from — scan the label on your invoice to see the source.

  • ↩️

    No-fuss returns

    Not happy with ripeness or quality? We refund or replace the item, no questions, no receipts needed.
